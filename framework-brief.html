Aureon Executive Brief · Pre-Trade Governance

Pre-Trade Governance for AI-Assisted Investment Systems

Aureon sits between signal generation and execution infrastructure. Its role is to validate doctrine, risk, compliance, and human authority before a trade becomes a portfolio event.

What It Is

Governance Layer

The control layer that makes AI-assisted decisions explainable, reviewable, and auditable before execution. Not a broker, OMS, or portfolio management replacement.

Core Outcome

Decision Accountability

Every trade reconstructed as a lifecycle package: signal, doctrine check, risk review, compliance validation, human approval, execution, and settlement evidence.

Why It Matters

Smarter, Not Noisier

Financial systems made more intelligent by structuring intent and governance — not by adding disconnected workflow complexity or weakening supervisory control.

Lifecycle

How Aureon Works

01 —

Signal or Thesis Input

Neptune Spear originates. Market signal, portfolio drift, or predictive thesis with full analytical lineage.

02 —

Human Authority Review

Operator reviews Neptune output at CAOM-001 authority level. Approves, modifies, or rejects. No agent substitutes.

03 —

Doctrine + Risk Validation

Mentat checks doctrine boundaries. Kaladan structures lifecycle. Rules, thresholds, and regulatory frameworks evaluated visibly.

04 —

C2 Coordination + Execution

Thifur-C2 sequences R/J/H agents. Deterministic execution within bounded authority. One unified lineage record assembled.

05 —

DSOR + Replay

Execution, settlement, and audit artifacts stamped with CAOM-001 authority hash. Complete replayable record.

Gate Economics

The Cost of Governance vs. The Cost of None

Every doctrine gate has a latency cost and a tail risk it prevents. Aureon is not built for microsecond HFT execution. It is built for governed systematic execution where the regulatory and operational tail risk of an ungoverned action exceeds the alpha lost to a gate delay.

Gate Layer Latency Cost Tail Risk Prevented
Network node validation Verana Milliseconds Unregistered counterparty exposure — Lehman-style cascade
Doctrine boundary check Mentat Milliseconds MiFID II enforcement action — algo trading without compliant controls
Lifecycle approval lineage Kaladan Seconds SR 11-7 model risk violation — no replayable human authority record
C2 handoff authorization Thifur-C2 Milliseconds Fragmented agent telemetry — no unified lineage at audit time
Human authority approval CAOM-001 Human decision time Regulatory existential risk — no defensible authority trail when the regulator asks
Neptune origination review Neptune Spear Human decision time Market manipulation exposure — systematic models that move markets without human authority stamp
Positioning

Three Deployment Modes

01

Governance Overlay

Aureon sits above existing OMS infrastructure as a pre-trade governance and DSOR layer. No replacement of incumbent stack. Clean integration boundary. Immediate regulatory value.

02

Full-Stack Operating System

Greenfield deployment of the complete six-layer architecture — Neptune Spear through Thifur — as the primary governed financial operating system for a new fund or desk.

03

Strategy License

Individual named strategy modules licensed to institutional counterparties. Each license scoped to a specific strategy — not generic architecture access. Named per GWOT operation convention.
